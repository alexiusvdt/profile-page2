This is Susuwatari (Pronounced Soo-soo-wah-tah-ri). She is a little over two years old.

In the original Japanese, her name is written as ススワタリ meaning "wandering soot". This is a fictitious sprite invented by Hayao Miyazaki in his wonderful movies Spirited Away and My Neighbor Totoro. The sprites are shy but exciteable and have a very goofy appearance, just like my beloved fuzzball. Take a look at the images and compare!

Right: a cluster of sprites eating konpeitō, a traditional Japanese sugar candy
